License FAQ

How long does my trial period last?

Your trial lasts until the next time Live Studio is updated. For example. If you download v1.5, when v1.6 comes out your trial will be considered expired and you will have to purchase a license to continue to use the app.

Note: At the very minimum you will get a one week trial. This means if you purchased it a day or two before the next update, you will still have a fair amount of time to try out the app. Also, minor bug fix releases (e.g. 1.5.1) will not count against you.

Are there any limited features with the trial?

No. You get to try Live Studio in it’s entirety before deciding to purchase it.

What do I get by purchasing a license?

A license allows you to use Live Studio indefinitely, free of charge. It also allows you access to every future update without paying again.

How do I purchase a license?

You can purchase a license though in-app purchases within Live Studio. You are bound by the App Store terms when purchasing a license.

Why this instead of the traditional ad-supported freemium model?

This is really an experiment. I think our customers should be able to decide on their own if they believer Live Studio is worth paying for. This offers customers a no-risk trial to the entire app without feeling cheated if it doesn’t live up to expectations. Also this model incentives me, as the developer, to keep the app up to date with regular updates and features that give the user a reason to support the app.
